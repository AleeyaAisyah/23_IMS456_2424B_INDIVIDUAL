PROGRAM 1: STAR AMONG US

During my enriching journey at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Cawangan Kota Bharu, I actively engaged in extracurricular activities to make the most out of my college experience. One such endeavor that became close to my heart was joining the esteemed Kelab Inspirasi. Today, I proudly serve as Setiausaha Agung of Kelab Inspirasi, working alongside like-minded individuals to make a positive impact on the lives of others.

One of the most rewarding experiences of my time as a member of the club has been organizing the Star Among Us program. This initiative was thoughtfully designed to foster communication and understanding with the students of Special Integration Education Program (PPKI) from Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Sireh.

The program left an indelible mark on the lives of the PPKI students. Witnessing their enthusiasm and progress throughout the program was incredibly fulfilling fo me and the entire Kelab Inspirasi team. The initiative not only contributed to the development of the student's motor skills but also strengthened the bonds between the club members and the PPKI students.

PROGRAM 2: CONFIDENCE INJECTION WITH INSPIRASI

I am also proud to have been part of the dedicated team at Kelab Inspirasi, where we organized the impactful "Confidence Injection With Inspirasi" program. This initiative aimed to address the pressing issue of body shaming and its detrimental effects on mental health. The program featured a talk by Dr. Nazira binti Sadiron, the Chief Assistant Director of the National Anti-Drug Agency, who provided valuable insights into this concerning cultural phenomenon.

Personally, being part of the organizing team for these two program has been a transformative experience. I have developed valuable project management, communication, and leadership skills, which will be instrumental in my future endeavors, both academically and professionally. Thus, I remain committed to initiating more programs and initiatives that address critical issues and bring about positive change in society.
